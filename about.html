Born from Institutional Expertise

First Principle Asset Management was created by The Invictus Collective, a multi-family office serving ultra-high-net-worth families and institutional clients. For years, Christian Habitz and Rob Bower have used structured-note portfolios to engineer downside-aware income and custom exposures for a small number of private clients.

Those portfolios were built one client at a time, with direct access to banks, structured-product desks, and institutional due diligence. The result was a set of repeatable frameworks for using structured notes as portfolio enhancements rather than one-off trades.

First Principle exists to bring that same architecture to the advisory market. Instead of bespoke tickets, we design scalable SMAs and AMC portfolios that give advisors and their clients access to the same research, counterparties, and risk framework previously reserved for UHNW families—without channel conflict and with clear integration into existing 60/40 and model portfolios.
