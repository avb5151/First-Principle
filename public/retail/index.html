Portfolio Enhancement for a New Market Regime

First Principle Asset Management designs structured-note portfolios that convert market volatility into stable income and improved risk-adjusted returns. Our Solution: Turning Volatility Into Income

We design diversified portfolios of structured notes engineered to complement traditional equity and credit exposure. Returns are driven by volatility pricing and outcome-defined payoff structures—not by market timing or directional forecasts.

Volatility as a Source of Income

Our portfolios convert market volatility into consistent income by harvesting option premiums across diversified underlying indices. Payoff mechanics, not market direction, drive returns.

Higher Income with Defined Risk Parameters

Structured-note portfolios deliver elevated cash yields relative to IG and HY credit, without duration risk. Transparent barrier levels tied to broad indices provide clarity on potential outcomes.

Outcome-Driven Behavior Across Market Regimes

Structured-note payoffs behave differently from long-only assets, generating income in range-bound or moderately rising markets and adding asymmetric exposure that complements traditional portfolios.
